WeatherAirSN Dataset

WeatherAirSN is a comprehensive multi-source atmospheric dataset integrating ground observations, air quality monitoring, and ECMWF model data. This visualization platform demonstrates the dataset's reliability and consistency through three representative extreme weather events across different climate zones in China, validating the data quality and applicability for atmospheric research and machine learning applications.
